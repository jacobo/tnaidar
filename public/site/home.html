Kupenda (Swahili for "to love") for the children" is a 501 (c)(3)tax-exempt Christian charity that meets the physical, emotional and spiritual needs of handicapped children in the developing world. Currently the focus of Kupenda is on the coast of Kenya but is expanding to other countries.
